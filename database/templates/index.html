Kindergargen Vaccination Database

By John C. Osborn

{% include 'social.html' %}

Use this database to find out how many kindergarten students were vaccinated at any public, charter or private California elementary school in the 2015-16 school year. California law mandates that kindergarten students be up-to-date on a number of vaccines that help prevent communicable childhood diseases, such as whooping cough, measles and polio. However, the 2015-16 school year is the last one which will allow parents to opt out their children from those vaccine requirements for religious or personal reasons after the passage of SB 277 in 2015. Read our answers to "frequently asked questions" about how SB 277 changes vaccine requirements.

Have a question about the database? Send your questions and comments our way.

Search Elementary Schools

Search through our list of over 7,000 elementary schools below to learn about their vaccine rates. Only schools designated as currently operating by the California Department of Education for the 2015-16 school year are listed below. Only schools with 10 or more students are listed in our database.
